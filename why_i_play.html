WHY I PLAY.

There are a bunch of reasons why someone plays games, some might play games because it's a great way to connect with friends and be able to spend time with them online if you are not able to spend time in person because of the coronavirus. Or people might play to find new friends and meet great people. 

Some people might take up story games or single-player games instead of playing multiplayer games, and for good reason. Video games can be beautiful and tell amazing stories and touch the hearts of anyone playing it. Some great examples of that are the videos on YouTube titled It's just a video game, an amazing one that I've watched multiple times is this one by Palentro. It should be known that if you do watch that video and play video games, please check the description for the list of games shown in the video to avoid potential spoilers.

For me, I play to make memories and have fun with friends. I'm not going to deny that I play to feel a rush of adrenaline from winning and performing round winning clutches and whatnot but I mainly play to have fun in the end.
