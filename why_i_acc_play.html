WHY I ACTUALLY PLAY.

So I could tell you why I play and say I play because I enjoy playing with friends and living moments and laughing at funny things while with them, and of course that's all true, but I also like winning.

Everyone and their mother likes winning, there is nobody I know who likes to lose. If you like to lose, no you don't, stop lying to yourself. I got into competitive play and tournaments because I like winning and I dislike losing, as most people should. Of course, I can't do that unless I play with friends who I know are good as well, so I guess I do play because I like playing with friends, and I like playing with friends because we win, and I like winning and so do my friends, so it's a win-win all around.

I also like playing because I get a lot of good clips and funny clips, most of which I can show here because I can't take up space in the servers I guess.
